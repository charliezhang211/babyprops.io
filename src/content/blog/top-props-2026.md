## Building Your 2026 Prop Collection

The new year brings fresh trends and timeless classics to the world of newborn photography. Whether you're just starting out or looking to refresh your collection, these are the must-have props for 2026.

## The Essential Top 10

### 1. Wooden Moon Bed

The iconic moon bed remains a client favorite. Its curved design creates beautiful silhouettes and works with any color scheme.

**Why it's essential:**
- Timeless appeal
- Versatile styling options
- Supports babies up to 8kg

### 2. Neutral Wrap Collection

A set of high-quality wraps in neutral tones forms the backbone of any prop collection.

**Must-have colors:**
- Cream
- Soft gray
- Oatmeal
- Blush

### 3. Rattan Basket Set

Rattan baskets have surged in popularity for their organic, bohemian aesthetic.

**Trending styles:**
- Round baskets
- Moses basket shapes
- Woven textures

### 4. Mohair Bonnets

Delicate mohair bonnets add the perfect finishing touch to any setup.

**Popular styles:**
- Bear ear bonnets
- Classic knit bonnets
- Flower-adorned pieces

### 5. Posing Bean Bag

While not photographed directly, a quality posing beanbag is essential for safety and versatility.

**Features to look for:**
- Waterproof cover
- Sturdy filling
- Large enough for composite work

### 6. Floral Headbands

Floral accessories remain timeless for baby girls, with more subtle, natural looks trending in 2026.

**2026 trends:**
- Dried flower styles
- Small, delicate pieces
- Earth-toned flowers

### 7. Knitted Outfits

Hand-knitted rompers and outfits photograph beautifully and add texture to images.

**Popular pieces:**
- Cable knit rompers
- Lace detail sets
- Matching hat sets

### 8. Wooden Props

Natural wooden props continue to dominate:

- Mini beds
- Heart-shaped bowls
- Letter blocks

### 9. Backdrop Collection

Quality backdrops are worth the investment:

- Stretch jersey in neutral tones
- Textured fabrics
- Faux fur for variety

### 10. Storage and Organization

Don't forget about prop storage:

- Clear bins for visibility
- Labels for quick access
- Climate-controlled space

## Investment Tips

### Where to Splurge
- Quality posing equipment (safety first!)
- Versatile neutral pieces
- Well-made wooden props

### Where to Save
- Seasonal accent pieces
- Trend-specific items
- Backup wraps

## Conclusion

Building a prop collection is a journey, not a destination. Start with versatile classics, add trends thoughtfully, and always prioritize quality and safety.
