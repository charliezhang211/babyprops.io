Our Premium Mohair Wraps are a must-have staple for every newborn photographer. Hand-knitted from the finest mohair blend, these wraps offer incredible softness, beautiful drape, and the perfect amount of stretch for effortless posing.

## Product Details

The Warm Cream color is a timeless neutral that works beautifully with any skin tone and complements all our props and accessories. The open-weave knit creates gorgeous texture in photos while remaining soft and gentle against delicate newborn skin.

## Why Choose Mohair?

- **Superior Softness**: Mohair is one of the softest natural fibers
- **Perfect Stretch**: Excellent elasticity for easy wrapping and posing
- **Beautiful Texture**: Creates depth and interest in photographs
- **Lightweight**: Won't add bulk or weight to your setups

## Styling Tips

- Layer with other wraps for added dimension
- Use as a backdrop or base layer
- Perfect for swaddle wrapping techniques
- Pairs beautifully with wooden props
- Works for both boys and girls

## Technical Specifications

- **Material**: 60% Mohair, 40% Acrylic blend
- **Size**: 150cm x 40cm
- **Knit Type**: Open-weave for texture
- **Stretch**: 4-way stretch
- **Weight**: Lightweight (approximately 100g)

Available in multiple colorways to coordinate with your studio's aesthetic. Each wrap is individually hand-knitted to ensure consistent quality.
