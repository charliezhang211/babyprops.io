## Care & Storage

To maintain the beauty of your floral crowns:
- Store in the included protective box
- Gently reshape flowers if they become compressed
- Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading
- Handle by the base to avoid crushing delicate blooms

Each crown is made to order with attention to detail. While flowers are consistent in style and color palette, slight variations make each piece unique, just like real wildflowers.

**Note**: These are photography props designed for brief wear during photo sessions. Always supervise babies when using any photography accessories.
